WELCOME TO CHRISTCHURCH CITY HOTEL

OPENED JULY 2021

Located in the heart of the revitalised and vibrant city centre, Christchurch City Hotel offers stylish accommodation, friendly service, and stunning city views. Architecturally designed and built to the highest safety standards with both quality and comfort in mind.

Spend your visit with us exploring Christchurch while enjoying the convenience of the very best in location, location, location. From your central base you will be within easy walking distance of the cities attractions, or wanting something a little different, let our team arrange an e-scooter or electric bike. If you are venturing further afield, we have valet parking available. If you are staying for business, simply stroll out the front door with your espresso in hand before heading to your next meeting, conference or function. It couldn't be easier.

As Christchurch's newest hotel we offer 46 rooms ranging from our Executive Studio to Executive Suites featuring contemporary furnishings, modern stylish colour schemes, air conditioning, work desks, WiFi, tea and coffee making facilities and smart televisions with streaming services. Our skyline rooftop bar features local artisan produce and Canterbury wines is due to open for your gastronomic enjoyment in Summer 2023.

We look forward to welcoming you to Christchurch City Hotel where our friendly team will ensure your stay is relaxed and will have you already looking forward to your next visit back to Christchurch.

Christchurch City Hotel is a must for your next visit. Our friendly team look forward to welcoming you to stay with us in the heart of Christchurch.
